How It All Began

Our Story

“

Jerv and Maine met at university, two strangers whose paths crossed and soon after, quietly intertwined.

What began as a simple friendship grew into a deep and enduring love. Since 2012, they have been inseparable, sharing life side by side as best friends first. Different in many ways yet alike where it mattered, they found balance in each other. What one loved, the other came to cherish, and in time, they found a rhythm that was entirely their own.

Through the years, their journey has not been without challenges. There were moments that tested their strength, yet they chose each other, again and again.

After more than a decade of growing together, Jerv asked Maine to spend a lifetime with him, and she said yes.

On December 22, they begin their next chapter, promising a lifetime together.
