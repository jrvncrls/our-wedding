Dress Code

Formal Attire

We invite you to dress elegantly for our special day. Think floor-length gowns, cocktail dresses, or smart suits. We want everyone to feel their most beautiful.

Please Do
  • Floor-length or tea-length gowns for ladies
  • Suits or barong tagalog for gentlemen
  • Elegant cocktail dresses
  • Smart formal separates
Please Avoid
  • White, ivory, or cream (reserved for the bride)
  • Casual wear, jeans, or shorts
  • Flip-flops or athletic footwear
